About Us

As Georgian students, we understand the frustrations that come along with coding. Countless hours spent debugging can be incredibly annoying. That's why we're here!

Our development team, 3-Bits, has taken it upon ourselves to create a coding forum where Georgian students can collaborate, share, and problem-solve as a united community.

Our Mission

At 3-Bits, our mission is to foster a supportive environment for Georgian students interested in coding. We aim to provide a platform where you can:

  • Collaborate with fellow students on coding projects.
  • Share your knowledge and experiences.
  • Seek help and solutions for coding challenges.
  • Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for technology.
